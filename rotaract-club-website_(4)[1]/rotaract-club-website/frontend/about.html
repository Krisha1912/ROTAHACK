From humble beginnings to community impact, Rotaract's history is a tapestry of service, innovation, and friendships that endure.

Rotary began as the dream of Paul Harris, a Chicago-based attorney, who founded the first Rotary Club on February 23, 1905. His goal was to bring together professionals from varied backgrounds to foster idea-sharing and cultivate lasting, meaningful friendships. As the movement grew, so did its purpose ‚Äî evolving into a global force for humanitarian service.
