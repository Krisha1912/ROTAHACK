Rotaract: A Revolution of Kindness

Rotary is a global network of more than 1.2 million neighbors, friends, leaders, and problem-solvers who see a world where people unite and take action to create lasting change – across the globe, in our communities, and in ourselves.

We don’t wait for change. We become it.

Rotary began as the dream of Paul Harris, a Chicago-based attorney, who founded the first Rotary Club on February 23, 1905. His goal was to bring together professionals from varied backgrounds to foster idea-sharing and cultivate lasting, meaningful friendships. As the movement grew, so did its purpose — evolving into a global force for humanitarian service. Rotary members have consistently stepped up to tackle pressing issues both locally and across the globe, leaving behind a legacy of compassion, leadership, and community-driven impact.
